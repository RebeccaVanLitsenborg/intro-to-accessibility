"Where the sky meets the sea is where I'm meant to be..."

Cornwall, United Kingdom...

This is where I grew up. Everytime I go home, I am still amazed that I got to grow up here. Cornwall has an amazing history and it's so much fun to explore, especially when you know all the secret hidden spots. The cliffs are dotted with caves and old smugglers tunnels and the surf is amazing, if not a little scary. Growing up here was probably the reason why I am so drawn to the sea and it definitley contributes to my passion for water sports.
