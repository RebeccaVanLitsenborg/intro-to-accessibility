CapeTown, South Africa...

This was probably the biggest adventure I have ever been on. I took a one way flight to CapeTown and stayed there for an entire year. I had just turned 18 and didn't realise I was only allowed to stay for 90 days so when I went to fly home I was grilled about my intentions and the reason for my extended visit. I am not sure if I looked terrified or they could just see I was pretty young but eventually they let me get on my flight. But not before choosing to either pay a fine, or have an official 5 year ban from South Africa! The trip itself was unbelievable. I felt so at home, the beaches and wildlife were amazing and I will never forget the food. South Africans are serious meat eaters! It took me a long long time to stop feeling sad after I had to fly back home.
