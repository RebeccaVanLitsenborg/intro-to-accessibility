"Where the sky meets the sea is where I'm meant to be..."

Hi, I'm Becca

I have been passionate about travelling from an early age and love to explore new places. I grew up by the sea and my parents always used to joke that I was born half mermaid. I love being by the ocean as well as in it, and I am a bit of a sun (heat) worshipper. I really struggle with the cold but I try not to let that stop me from exploring colder places. Iceland is top of my bucket list followed closely by Scotland! I will have to dress like an Eskimo but my biggest travel dream is to see the Northern Lights. I also really want to visit Thailand so I can get my diving certificate somewhere a bit warmer than here! My favourite water sports are windsurfing, surfing, and sailing but I havnt done much of that since moving to Belgium.
