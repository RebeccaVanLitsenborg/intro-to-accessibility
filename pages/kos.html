Kos, Greece...

I have been coming to this beautiful Island for the last 11 years. I consider it my second home and have gotten to know a lot of the locals quite well. One summer I stayed for 6 months and worked as a windsurfing instructor on Porto Bello beach. The memories I have of this place are irreplaceable and the second I step off that plane and get my first gulp of warm Greek air, I am the happiest person alive. I am terrified of volcanos but I have managed to get past that over the years, and now the view of Nisyros from Kardamena is one of my favourite views. I have been there...but once was enough!
